Research Interests

My research interests lie in developing fast and accurate algorithms to solve PDEs and real-world problems arising in image processing and machine learning. Below is the list of my research interests.

  • PDE-based machine learning and image processing
  • Optimization
  • Optimal transport
  • Gradient flows
  • Mean field games
  • Optimal control
